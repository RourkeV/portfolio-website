Introduction to Global Minor Program

Acting in IT with International Impact

The Global Acting in IT minor brings together students from across the world to collaborate on real-life projects with international impact. Over the course of one semester, participants explore five key IT domains, Immersive Technology, UX Design, Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science, while working on a stakeholder project linked to the UN Sustainable Development Goals. The program combines technical learning with intercultural development, teamwork, and global citizenship, preparing students to act responsibly and creatively in a rapidly changing digital world.
